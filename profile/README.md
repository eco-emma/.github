
# EMMA: Ecological Monitoring, Mapping & Analysis

EMMA is an environmental intelligence initiative focused on building near real-time tools for monitoring ecosystem condition, detecting environmental change, and supporting evidence-based conservation and land management decisions. Developed in partnership with the South African Environmental Observation Network (SAEON), EMMA integrates NASA satellite Earth observation data, environmental datasets, field observations, ecological forecasting, and machine learning to transform environmental data into actionable insights.

## What We Do

- Monitor vegetation condition and ecosystem health using remote sensing and environmental data.
- Detect anomalies, ecosystem change, and disturbance events in near real time. 
- Assess fire impacts, vegetation damage, invasive species, and plant mortality across fire-prone landscapes. 
- Develop ecological forecasting and decision-support capabilities for conservation practitioners, researchers, and environmental agencies.

## Research & Technology

Our work combines Earth observation, ecological modeling, deep learning, automated data processing pipelines, and decision-support tools to deliver scalable environmental monitoring systems. Current efforts emphasize operational workflows that support environmental reporting, biodiversity monitoring, and ecosystem management. 

## Mission

We believe environmental decisions are strongest when informed by timely, transparent, and scientifically rigorous data. EMMA's goal is to make ecological intelligence accessible, actionable, and impactful for environmental stewardship and resilience.
